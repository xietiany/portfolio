Risk Quantitative Analyst

After graduating from the University of Michigan in July 2020, I joined Citi. Over the course of four years at the company, I made significant contributions to three key quantitative risk models: DSFT, IDL, and GSST retail. Each of these models was designed to address a specific set of risks, spanning market risk, counterparty risk, default risk, and the forecasting of potential losses under extreme stress scenarios. My involvement in these projects allowed me to deepen my understanding of risk management practices and refine my skills in quantitative modeling and analysis within the dynamic financial landscape of Citi.
